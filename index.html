Privacy Policy for Receipt Splitter

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Receipt Splitter ("the app") is a bill-splitting app that lets you create trips, scan or enter receipts, and split costs with a group. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and who it is shared with.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: your email address, and, if you sign in with Apple or Google, the name and email address those services provide.
  • Trip and expense data you enter: trip names, the names of people you add to a trip, receipt items, prices, tax, tip, and manual expenses. This data is stored so it can sync across your devices and be shared with the other members of a trip you create or join.
  • Receipt photos: if you scan a receipt, the photo is processed to extract items and prices (see "Receipt Scanning" below) and a copy is saved locally on your device. We do not upload or store receipt photos on our servers.
  • Subscription status: whether you have an active Pro subscription, to unlock paid features.

We do not use advertising or analytics SDKs, and we do not sell your information to third parties.

Receipt Scanning

When you scan a receipt, the photo is sent from your device to our server, which forwards it to OpenAI's API to extract the restaurant name, items, prices, tax, and tip as text. The photo is used only to perform this extraction and is not stored by us or, to our knowledge, retained by OpenAI beyond what is needed to process the request. The extracted text is then saved as part of your trip data as described above.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and secure your account, and let you sign in across devices.
  • To let you create, join, and share trips with other people you invite.
  • To calculate balances and settlements between members of a trip.
  • To extract receipt details when you use the scan feature.
  • To provide and manage Pro subscription features.

Who We Share Information With

We share data only with the service providers needed to operate the app:

  • Supabase — hosts our database and handles authentication.
  • OpenAI — processes receipt photos to extract text, as described above.
  • Apple and Google — provide Sign in with Apple / Google Sign-In if you choose to use them.
  • Apple's In-App Purchase / StoreKit system — processes subscription payments; we do not receive or store your payment card details.

Other members of a trip you join can see the trip, expense, and receipt data associated with that trip, since the app is designed for shared bill-splitting.
